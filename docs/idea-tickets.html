Projects · Phase 1 & Phase 2

Idea Tickets

Before you build anything, you submit an idea ticket. Your instructor approves it — then you start. No ticket = no building. This prevents scope creep and keeps you on track.

⛔ Approval Gates — Why These Exist
  • Phase 1 (Day 3): Idea ticket approved → then you start building your personal site
  • Phase 2 (Day 9): Idea ticket approved by end of class → then you start building your interactive project
  • Ideas that are too big get scoped down. Ideas that are too vague get clarified. The ticket saves you from building the wrong thing.
